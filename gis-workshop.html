GIS Week 2018: GIS Workshop

“Implementing GIS into Real Life with ArcGIS Platforms” is this year's theme for GIS Workshop. We have designed a workshop to teach you how to learn about developing apps using ArcGIS APIs and SDKs. You will be able to discover the latest GIS technology and do hands-on with two of our best Esri Indonesia trainers guiding you through. You can then go ahead and build your own apps to solve real life problems.

Highlights :
  • Opening remarks from Esri Inc. Michael Gould
  • Explore Esri SDK
  • Esri with JavaScript API
  • Custom Mobile Apps Hands-on
