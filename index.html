What Is GIS Week ?

GIS Week is a creative event held once a year that aims to introduce Geographic Information System technology, a technology that has already been utilized worldwide, to Indonesian Communities.

The theme of this year’s GIS Week is Geographical Information System Application in Big Data Era.
