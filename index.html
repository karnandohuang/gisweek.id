What Is GIS Week ?

GIS Week is a creative event held once a year that aims to introduce Geographic Information System technology, a technology that has already been utilized worldwide, to Indonesian Communities.

The theme of this year’s GIS Week is Geographical Information System Application in Big Data Era.

Our Events

October 24th and 26th, 2018

Auditorium BINUS University Alam Sutera

“GIS The Golden Thread of Industry of 4.0 and Transformation of Technology in Indonesia“ and “Big Data, A Big Driver in The Future of Actionable Intelligence“ are the theme for this year's GIS Talkshow.
This talkshow is an event where experts from public, private and technology sector, and academia come together to discuss some of the most important topics of this era. Our speakers will talk about the challenges of the Industrial Revolution 4.0, Smart Data Driven processes, and the potential of Geo Artificial Intelligent for automating workflow.

October 24th, 2018

Auditorium BINUS University Alam Sutera

“Location Intelligence Solution” is the theme of this year's GIS Hacks. GIS Hacks is a competition among students or fresh graduate between 15 to 25 years old. The competition is open to teams throughout Indonesia. Registration and Prototype must be submitted before October 10th, 2018. Our judges will select five best teams to present in BINUS University and get a chance to be hired by reputable companies. The winners will be announced on the closing ceremony on October 26th, 2018.

October 26th, 2018

Auditorium BINUS University Alam Sutera

“Implementing GIS into Real Life with ArcGIS Platforms” is this year's theme for GIS Workshop. We have designed a workshop to teach you how to learn about developing apps using ArcGIS APIs and SDKs. Auditorium BINUS University Alam Sutera

GIS Industry Forum is a highly-interactive event that offers valuable opportunities to connect, network, and collaborate with fellow decision-makers and GIS expert in your industry.
